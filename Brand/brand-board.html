Design Concept

Symbol Meaning

Three flowing curves represent financial journeys tracked over time, with varying opacity suggesting depth and layers of data. The dot marks the current position—your financial "now."

Flow & Direction

Curved paths suggest forward movement and financial progress without aggressive upward arrows

Layered Tracking

Multiple curves with varied opacity represent different aspects of financial data working together

Calm Control

Soft, rounded geometry creates a sense of trust and approachability, not corporate rigidity

Present Moment

The tracking dot represents "where you are now" — mindful awareness of your current financial state
